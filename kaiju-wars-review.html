Kaiju Wars has stomped its way into the strategy gaming scene with a roar, bringing a brilliant sense of style, a distinct setting, and tight tactical gameplay. If you're a fan of strategy games or anything kaiju, monstrous, and wondrous, Kaiju Wars deserves a prime spot in your gaming library.

A Unique Blend of Classic Influences

Imagine tossing Into The Breach and Advance Wars into a nuclear reactor with the contents of an abandoned VHS store. What emerges is Kaiju Wars - a brilliant turn-based strategy game that masterfully combines nostalgic elements with fresh, innovative gameplay. It's all about managing the seemingly inevitable kaiju destruction while devising clever tactics to protect your city.

Commanding the Army

As the mayor of Floatio City, you are tasked with holding off the overwhelming strength of kaiju attacks. This game challenges you to scramble jets, deploy tanks, and park them in the path of these giant monsters. Your goal? Slow them down, distract them, and buy precious time. Fortunately, the city has a good insurance policy to cover these sacrificial defenses.

  • Building Sacrificial Structures: Constructing army bases and airfields to lure the kaiju away from critical areas is crucial. Lining the city streets with fodder can significantly affect the outcome of each encounter.
  • Deploying Experimental Weapons: Conventional weapons are not enough to fend off these colossal threats. You'll need to use freeze rays, transforming mechas, and other experimental weapons to keep the kaiju at bay.
  • Evacuating in Time: With the chief scientist working on an anti-monster serum, evacuation plans are essential. Transport trucks, boats, or planes are ready to relocate critical personnel when the kaiju get too close.

Facing the Kaiju

Kaiju Wars features five unique kaiju, each specializing in different types of destruction. As you progress through the game, you'll tremble in fear as these monsters mutate and gain devastating abilities through mid-level mutations. While they can't be killed, kaiju can be slowed down and forced to fall back. Their predictable movements towards the nearest building allow you to strategize and use this information wisely.

An Engaging Campaign Mode

The story-based campaign mode of Kaiju Wars offers over 15 hours of gameplay, where you uncover the mystery behind frequent volcanic eruptions, tsunamis, and earthquakes. Embark on missions around the globe, test your wits in tactical puzzles, and command dozens of units in large-scale scenarios. Fend off repeated attacks while developing your city economically in city defense missions.

  • Upgrade Your ACES: Veteran ACE units accompany you throughout your campaign. Spend medals to upgrade and customize your top fighters, making them even more formidable against the kaiju.
  • Customize Your Project Deck: Unlock powerful Project cards, customize your deck, and bring your best strategy to compete on the online leaderboard.

Extra Features and Community Integration

Kaiju Wars goes beyond the typical single-player experience with features like Twitch Integration, allowing Twitch chat to act as the citizens, typing commands that appear during in-game events. The Local Versus Mode lets two players duke it out on the same computer (or online via Steam RemotePlay), with one playing as the kaiju and the other as the humans.

  • Create and Share Your Battles: The game includes a simple and easy-to-use map editor for crafting your own cities or scenarios. Share your creations online using Steam Workshop integration and participate in weekly challenges to compete for top spots on the leaderboards.

Conclusion

Being the mayor of the world's greatest city is no longer just about zoning bylaws. Kaiju Wars takes you on an exhilarating journey of strategy, destruction, and survival. With its engaging gameplay, unique kaiju battles, and extensive customization options, this game is hard to put down. Whether you're a fan of strategy games or Godzilla movies, Kaiju Wars has something to offer. Dive into the chaos, and good luck defending Floatio City!
